# Chrome Web Store listing draft

## Name
Scamly Beta – AI Email Scam Detector

## Summary
Scamly Beta scans visible email content in Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo Mail for scam and phishing signals. Optional Deep AI Check sends the visible email text to your configured Scamly backend after opt-in.

## Description
Scamly Beta helps people spot scam and phishing patterns inside opened emails.

What Scamly does
- Runs a local first-pass scan on visible email content
- Flags urgency, login theft, OTP theft, gift card scams, fake jobs, invoice fraud, crypto pitches, delivery scams, toll scams, and suspicious links
- Offers an optional Deep AI Check through your configured Scamly backend
- Lets beta users send feedback for misses and false positives

Supported sites
- Gmail
- Outlook
- Yahoo Mail

Beta note
Scamly is a decision-support tool, not a guarantee. It may miss scams or flag safe emails. Always verify payment requests, OTP requests, login prompts, and urgent account notices through a trusted channel.

## Privacy disclosure
Scamly reads visible email content on supported webmail pages to provide the user-facing scam detection feature. Local scanning stays in the browser. Deep AI Check is optional and only runs after the user opts in and configures a backend.

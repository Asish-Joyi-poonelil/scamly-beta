Scamly Beta Support

What this beta is for

Scamly Beta is meant to catch risky patterns early and collect real-world feedback. It can miss scams and sometimes flag normal emails. Users should always verify payment requests, login prompts, gift card requests, OTP requests, and urgent account notices through a trusted channel.

Need help?

Use the feedback page to report misses, false positives, or UI issues. Include the email type, what Scamly said, and what you expected instead.
